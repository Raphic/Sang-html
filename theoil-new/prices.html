Rolled or Stretched

If you choose to have your painting rolled, the canvas will be rolled in a tube and be sent to you. You can then take the painting to any frame company to have it finished. Where as, a stretched painting will arrive ready to be framed and hung. Please allow extra days for delivery if you choose to have it stretched. An additional service fee of $40 is required for the stretched service.



Our Recommendation

It's best that we shipped you in a rolled tube and you can have it stretch out at any local store such as Hobby Lobby. Doing this not only save you money on the extra shipping cost but it also allow you to choose type of frame that best fit your taste. There is no guarantee that your stretched oil painting portrait won't get damage by the shipper.



Number of Figures

"Figure" refers to the number of people, pets, buildings, etc. in a painting. A painting with 2 people and 1 pet would be "3 Figures". If you have a photo of a landscape scene, it would be considered one (1) figure.

All pricings are based on U.S.D. currency.
